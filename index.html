About Us

TABSIO INFRA HEIGHTS LLP is a leading manufacturer and supplier of premium-quality building materials, committed to redefining standards in the construction industry. Since our inception, we have focused on innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction, making us a trusted partner for builders, architects, and developers across the region. With state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and rigorous quality control processes, we produce bricks and building materials that meet the highest benchmarks for strength, durability, and environmental responsibility — ensuring your projects are built to last. Registered as a Limited Liability Partnership (LLPIN: AAC-8245) on 17 December 2014, the company is headquartered in Patna and is backed by a total obligation of contribution amounting to ₹75,00,000. Our leadership team, comprising Mrs. Sita Singh, Mr. Dhiraj Kumar Singh, and Mr. Rajan Kumar Singh, brings decades of experience and vision to the table. Currently, TABSIO INFRA HEIGHTS LLP is managing 7 active construction sites and holds an estimated net worth of ₹20 Crore, as of 2024. This strong financial foundation, combined with our focus on continuous growth, allows us to take on projects of all scales with unmatched efficiency. As per the latest filings with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), our most recent Statement of Accounts and Solvency was submitted for the financial year ending 31 December 2024, reflecting our commitment to transparency and compliance. From residential developments to large-scale infrastructure projects, TABSIO INFRA HEIGHTS LLP remains dedicated to building the future — brick by brick.
